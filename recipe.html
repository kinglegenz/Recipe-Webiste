Garlic Butter Steak

Juicy, tender, and bursting with rich herbed flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (1-inch thick)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and sear steaks for 3‚Äì4 minutes per side.
  3. Add butter, garlic, and thyme to the pan. Spoon the melted butter over the steak as it cooks.
  4. Remove from heat, rest for 5 minutes, then slice and serve with extra garlic butter drizzle.
